void sync()

When called on the server, schedules a BlockEntity sync to client.This will cause toClientTag to be called on theserver to generate the packet data, and then fromClientTag on the client to decode that data.

This is preferable to updateListeners because it does not cause entities to update their pathing as a side effect.
